    Emergency Calm

    You're safe. Take it one step at a time.

    Step 1

    Stop & Acknowledge

    Say to yourself: "I am feeling overwhelmed and that is okay." Simply naming the feeling reduces its intensity.

    Step 2

    Take 3 Deep Breaths

    Breathe in slowly through your nose… hold… breathe out through your mouth. Repeat 3 times.

    Breathe in
    Step 3

    Look Around

    Name 5 things you can see right now. This brings you back to the present moment.

    Step 4

    Grounding Touch

    Press your feet flat on the floor. Feel the chair beneath you. Press and hold your thumb below for 5 seconds.

    You made it!

    You've just used real skills to manage a stressful moment. You're stronger than you think. You've got this.
